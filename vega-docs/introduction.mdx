# What is Vega?

**Vega** is an open-source, AI-powered command-line interface built by [Raimic Labs](https://github.com/Raimic-Labs). It turns your terminal into an intelligent coding partner тАФ one that understands your intent, picks the right AI agent, and produces working code directly in your project directory.

> **Code at the speed of stars.** тЬж

Instead of copy-pasting from ChatGPT or switching between browser tabs, you simply open your terminal, run `vega`, describe what you want to build, and watch it happen.

## How It Works

1. **You type a prompt** тАФ natural language, plain English, as specific or vague as you want
2. **Vega routes to the right agent** тАФ it analyzes your intent and picks the best specialized agent
3. **The agent generates code** тАФ using the configured AI model, it produces complete, working files
4. **Files land in your directory** тАФ no clipboard needed; Vega writes directly to your filesystem
5. **Iterate naturally** тАФ follow up, refine, ask questions тАФ Vega keeps the context throughout
